## üõ† Usage for Students

* **Pre-Lecture Reading**: It is highly recommended to skim the relevant overheads before class to familiarize yourself with the themes covered, the equations and formalism employed, and the nomenclature cited.
* **Derivations**: While the slides provide the "skeletal" structure of our mathematical derivations, the full step-by-step proofs may be further developed live on the chalkboard. Use these overheads as a reference for the final expressions and assumptions. You should be able to perform the derivation yourselves.
* **References**: Many slides contain citations to primary research papers and books. Students are encouraged to look up these sources to understand the experimental context behind the data shown.
